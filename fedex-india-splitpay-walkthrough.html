🛣️ 6. End-to-End Demo Journey

1

Onboard Individual

Create party record for Ankush Kumar. A dedicated Level 2 party vault is provisioned and a virtual account number auto-assigned for inbound credit routing.

2

Onboard Company

Register RMZ SME as a company entity. Same orchestration as individual — party, vault, and VA created in a single API call, ready to receive funds.

3

Top Up Vault

Simulate a customer payment via RTA credit. The VA number in the narration routes the inbound credit directly to the correct party vault, updating the balance in real time.

4

Book Consignment

Create a payment contract with consignment attributes (weight, product type). A Level 3 transaction vault is created and the quoted shipment amount is ring-fenced from the party vault.

5

Amend Consignment

Customer confirms the final weight or billed amount. The engine calculates the delta between the original quote and the final invoice, moving only the difference — up or down.

6

Trigger Payout

PAY action is fired on the transaction vault. The split calculator runs LOWER/HIGHER logic against chargeableWeight, computes each party's share, and schedules independent settlement per stakeholder.

Onboard→ Top Up→ Book→ Amend→ Payout

🧮 8. Split Logic

➗

SPLIT_TIER

  • Weight crosses each slab boundary
  • Each slab adds its own fixed charge
  • Total = sum of all traversed slabs
📏

FLAT_TIER

  • Finds the single matching weight band
  • Applies that band's rate to full transaction
  • Simple, predictable single-band result
⬇️

LOWER

  • Runs both SPLIT and FLAT methods
  • Picks the cheaper result
  • Used for Transporter — cost protection
⬆️

HIGHER

  • Runs both SPLIT and FLAT methods
  • Picks the richer result
  • Used for Customs — duty never under-collected
✅

ALL

  • No comparison performed
  • Always paid unconditionally
  • Used for Insurer — mandatory coverage
🛡️

minAmount

  • Guaranteed floor for every party
  • Applied after LOWER/HIGHER selection
  • If computed < min, pay min — always

📋 9. Split Profiles — Standard vs Premium

📦

Standard Profile — 3 Parties

  • Transporter: SPLIT_TIER FIXED, payOnly LOWER · T+2 settlement · Slab charges ₹400/₹300/₹600/₹1,200/₹2,000
  • Customs: SPLIT + FLAT PERCENTAGE, payOnly HIGHER · Quarterly · 2–3.5% SPLIT, 2–7% FLAT
  • FedEx India (Platform): Residual 100% · Fortnightly

Use case: regular uninsured domestic or international goods. No insurance leg. Lower transporter slab charges and lower customs duty rates across all bands.

⭐

Premium Profile — 4 Parties

  • Transporter: Higher slab charges (~25% richer: ₹500/₹400/₹750/₹1,500/₹2,500) · T+1 settlement
  • Customs: Higher duty rates · 2.5–4.5% SPLIT, 2.5–9% FLAT · Quarterly
  • Insurer: FLAT_TIER, payOnly ALL · 0.75–1.5% of TXN · Weekly (Mon) — always paid
  • FedEx India (Platform): Residual 100% · Fortnightly

Use case: fragile, high-value, or insured cargo. Adds a mandatory 4th party (Insurer) on every shipment. Richer payout rates across all parties. Same orchestration path — different profile.

Standard → 3 parties · Premium → 4 parties. The extra party (Insurer) is always paid on every Premium transaction regardless of method comparison. Higher transporter slab charges and higher customs rates in Premium. KEY DIFF

✨ 13. Product Features Shown in the Demo

🏦

Vault per Client

Each onboarded party gets a dedicated, trackable vault. Individual and company clients are fully isolated from each other.

🔢

VA-based Routing

Credits and debits map cleanly to the right vault via virtual account number — no manual reconciliation needed.

📁

Transaction Vaults

Each consignment gets its own ring-fenced vault. Funds are locked per shipment before payout is triggered.

🧭

Policy Selection

Standard vs Premium is chosen from productType / productCategory payload attributes — no separate config call needed.

🧮

Method Comparison

LOWER and HIGHER guards are fully automatic. The engine runs both SPLIT and FLAT methods and picks the correct result every time.

🛡️

Minimum Payouts

Guaranteed floor for every party. If formula gives less than minAmount, the floor is paid — always, on every transaction, not just edge cases.

♻️

Residual Closure

FedEx India receives the exact residual amount. Every rupee in the transaction is distributed — zero leakage guaranteed mathematically.

📆

Settlement Scheduling

Each party follows its own independent settlement cycle — T+1, T+2, Weekly, Quarterly, or Fortnightly — all from one payment event.

One shipment event fans out into multiple independently scheduled payouts. Full traceability from customer credit to every downstream settlement. UPP VALUE
